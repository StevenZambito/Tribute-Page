Lady Gaga

One of the most sucessful female artists of all time

Lady Gaga at the 2019 GRAMMY's holding her newly won awards for Best Song Written for Visual Media, Best Pop Duo/Group Performance, and Best Pop Solo Performance.

Here is a timeline of Lady Gaga's career achievements:

  • 2011 - Achieves her first No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 with Born This Way, which sold over one million copies worldwide in its first week of release.
  • 2012 - Wins three Grammy awards, one of which being Best Pop Vocal Album for 2011's Born This Way.
  • 2013 - Achieves her second No. 1 album on the Billbaord 200 with ARTPOP
  • 2014 - Achieves her third No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 alongside Tony Bennett with Cheek to Cheek
  • 2016 - Achieves her fourth No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 with Joanne
  • 2016 - Wins a Golden Globe for her performance in American Horror Story: Hotel
  • 2018 - Stars alongside Bradley Cooper in her first film A Star is Born, grossing over $435 million worldwide at the box office.
  • 2018 - Achieves her fifth No. 1 album on the Billbaord 200 alongside Bradley Cooper with the A Star is Born Soundtrack
  • 2019 - Wins an Academy Award (Oscar) for Best Original Song for "Shallow" from the A Star is Born Soundtrack
  • 2020 - Awarded two more Grammy's from her work on the A Star is Born Soundtrack, bringing her total number of Grammy wins since the start of her career to 12, as of February 2020.
