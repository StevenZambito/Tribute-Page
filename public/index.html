Lady Gaga

One of the most sucessful female artists of all time

Lady Gaga at the 2019 GRAMMY's holding her newly won awards for Best Song Written for Visual Media, Best Pop Duo/Group Performance, and Best Pop Solo Performance.


Here is a timeline of Lady Gaga's career achievements:


  • 1986 - Born in NYC
  • 2011 - Achieves her 1st No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 with Born This Way which went on to sell over 1 million copies in it's first week of release.
  • 2013 - Achieves her 2nd No. 1 album on the Billbaord 200 with ARTPOP
  • 2014 - Achieves her 3rd No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 alongside Tony Bennet with Cheek to Cheek
  • 2016 - Achieves her 4th No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 with Joanne
  • 2016 - Wins a Golden Globe for her performance in American Horror Story: Hotel
  • 2018 - Stars alongside Bradley Cooper in her first film, A Star is Born grossing over $435 million worldwide at the box office.
  • 2018 - Achieves her 5th No. 1 album on the Billbaord 200 alongside Bradley Cooper with the A Star is Born Soundtrack
  • 2019 - Wins an Academy Award for Best Original Song for "Shallow" from A Star is Born Soundtrack
